# North America Sales Dashboard

![North America Sales Dashboard](Screenshot-2025-08-18-201941.jpg)

An **interactive Power BI dashboard** built on the **Superstore sales dataset** to analyze North America retail performance.  
This project showcases **revenue, cost, profit, quantity, delivery rate, and average delivery days**, along with breakdowns by customer, state, and product segments.

---

## Tools & Technologies

- **Power BI** – Data modeling, DAX measures, interactive visuals  
- **Excel / CSV** – Source data storage and preprocessing  
- **Git & GitHub** – Version control and project documentation  

---

## Business Objectives

- Provide a **single-page executive view** of North America retail performance.  
- Highlight **top customers, states, and product sub-categories** driving revenue and profit.  
- Analyze **return rates and shipping modes** to improve logistics and customer experience.  

---

## Dashboard Highlights

- **KPI cards** for Revenue, Cost, Profit, Quantity, Delivery Rate, and Avg Delivery Days.  
- **Customer Details (Top 15)** table with revenue, quantity, orders, and profit.  
- **Revenue by State** using a filled map and a **Top 10 states bar chart**.  
- **Revenue by Sub-Category** bar chart to identify best-performing product areas.  
- **Return Rate by Ship Mode** line chart to compare performance of shipping types.  
- **Filter panel** with slicers for **Year, Region, Segment, and Category**.
